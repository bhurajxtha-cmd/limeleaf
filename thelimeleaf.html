Our Story

Where
Thailand meets
Hampshire

✦

Set in a historic part of Basingstoke known as the 'Top of Town', The Lime Leaf brings an authentic mixture of history and fine Thai cuisine together under one roof.

Using our Thai roots, we've created a restaurant with vibrant decor and atmosphere designed to transport you — so you feel like you've found your very own piece of Thailand, right here in the heart of Hampshire.

We pride ourselves on delicious food and excellent customer service, and we're often described as one of the best restaurants in Basingstoke.
